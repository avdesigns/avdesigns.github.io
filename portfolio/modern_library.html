Study: A Modern Library Website

Modern library is a non-commercial study on modern website design.

Modern Library is a library website to relax and browse. It resembles the open spaciousness and natural light of the physical library, allowing the books and information to breathe.

Essential services are easy to find, and digital book displays are reimagined in bite-sized chunks. Let the book covers shine! It’s a quiet online space to explore the digital stacks.
